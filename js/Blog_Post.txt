Pycon India 2009

Pycon India 2009 is a community conference by the Python India developer community.

Pycon India 2009 would be held in Bangalore, India, between Saturday, 26th
September 2009 and Sunday, 27th September 2009 consisting of tutorials, talks
and short sprint sessions.

Register for PyCon India 2009: http://in.pycon.org/

Submit a Talk Proposal: http://in.pycon.org/2009/addtalk/
